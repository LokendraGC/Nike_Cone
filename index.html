Air Force 1 shoes, often referred to as AF1 or simply Air Force, are a popular line of athletic shoes produced by Nike. Originally released in 1982, these iconic sneakers were designed by Bruce Kilgore and named after the aircraft that carries the President of the United States, the Air Force One. The Air Force 1 was the first basketball shoe to feature Nike's innovative Air cushioning technology, providing enhanced comfort and impact absorption.
